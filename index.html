Australian Women and WWI

To what extent did women's voices influence change during and after WWI?

Intro Roles Change After WWI Conclusion

Introduction

Australian women played important roles during WWI through nursing, work, and activism.

Women During WWI

Women worked as nurses, volunteers, and took jobs while men were at war.

Influencing Change

Women influenced political debate, especially around conscription and social reform.

After WWI

Women gained more independence but inequality remained.

Conclusion

Women significantly influenced change, but it was gradual and incomplete.
